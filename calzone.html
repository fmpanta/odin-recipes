Calzone

Description

Restaurant-worthy Italian dinner in your kitchen.

Here is a nice recipe for the best calzone of your life.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Ricotta
  • Pepperoni
  • Mushrooms
  • Basil

Steps

  1. Make the dough
  2. Turn dough onto a lightly floured surface
  3. Make the filling
  4. Preheat oven
  5. Punch down dough
  6. Fold dough over filling
  7. Bake
